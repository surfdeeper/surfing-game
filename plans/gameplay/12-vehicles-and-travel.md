# Vehicles & Travel

Purpose: acquire vehicles to transport boards and travel to different surf destinations, unlocking progression opportunities.

## Vehicle Types

### Bikes

**Beach Cruiser** - $
- Carries: 1 shortboard (under arm)
- Speed: slow
- Range: local beach access only
- Vibe: chill, no parking hassles
- Unlocks: nothing new, just free transport

**Bike with Rack** - $$
- Carries: 1-2 boards (side rack)
- Speed: moderate
- Range: nearby beaches (5 mile radius)
- Vibe: eco-friendly, fitness bonus
- Perk: +small cardio boost per ride

### Cars

**Beater Sedan** - $$
- Carries: 2 boards (hang out windows/trunk)
- Speed: normal
- Range: regional (50 miles)
- Vibe: sketchy but functional
- Risk: boards can get dinged, car breaks down randomly

**Surf Wagon** - $$$
- Carries: 4 boards (roof rack)
- Speed: normal
- Range: regional
- Vibe: classic woody aesthetic
- Perk: looks cool, +style points

**Truck with Camper Shell** - $$$$
- Carries: 6+ boards
- Speed: normal
- Range: regional + overnight trips
- Vibe: dawn patrol ready
- Perk: sleep at the beach, first waves

### Vans

**Conversion Van** - $$$$$
- Carries: 8+ boards (full quiver)
- Speed: slow but steady
- Range: cross-country road trips
- Vibe: van life, freedom
- Perks:
  - Live at the beach (no rent costs)
  - Bring friends (multiplayer bonus)
  - Cook meals (diet flexibility)
  - Extended surf trips

**Sprinter Van (Luxury)** - $$$$$$$
- Carries: 10+ boards
- Speed: moderate
- Range: anywhere on the continent
- Vibe: Instagram van life goals
- Perks:
  - All conversion van perks
  - Solar power (charge devices)
  - Hot water (post-surf shower)
  - +comfort recovery bonus

### Boats

**Dinghy with Outboard** - $$$$
- Carries: 2-3 boards, 2 people
- Range: local offshore breaks, secret spots
- Speed: slow, weather dependent
- Vibe: scrappy explorer
- Unlocks: outer reefs, boat-access-only breaks

**Fishing Boat** - $$$$$$
- Carries: 4-6 boards, 4 people
- Range: offshore islands (day trips)
- Speed: moderate
- Vibe: salty, working boat
- Unlocks: island chains, remote points

**Yacht / Charter** - $$$$$$$$$$
- Carries: 10+ boards, crew + friends
- Range: international destinations
- Speed: fast, or anchor and stay
- Vibe: luxury surf charter
- Perks:
  - Access exclusive remote breaks
  - XP multiplier (pristine uncrowded waves)
  - Chef on board (perfect diet)
  - Spearfishing mini-game
- Unlocks: Indo, Fiji, Maldives, Mentawais

**Jet Ski** - $$$$$
- Carries: 1 board (tow-in setup)
- Range: local big wave spots
- Speed: fast
- Vibe: big wave hunter
- Unlocks: tow-in surfing at XXL spots
- Perk: rescue capability (save other surfers)

### Air Travel

**Commercial Flights** - $$ per trip
- Carries: 2 boards (board bag fees)
- Range: worldwide
- Speed: fast but hassle
- Cons: board damage risk, jet lag
- Unlocks: international destinations

**Private Plane (End Game)** - $$$$$$$$$$$
- Carries: unlimited boards
- Range: anywhere
- Speed: instant
- Vibe: pro surfer / tech bro money
- Perk: no board damage, no jet lag debuff

## Destinations & Progression

- Home break
- Nearby beaches
- XP: 1x multiplier (standard)

- Road trip spots (hours away)

- XP: 1.25x multiplier
- Better wave variety

### Offshore Tier (Boat)

- XP: 1.5x multiplier
- Uncrowded, pristine conditions

### Island Tier (Yacht/Charter)
- Remote island chains
- World-class setups
- XP: 2x multiplier
- Perfect waves, exotic locations

## Travel Mechanics

### Trip Planning
- Choose destination
- Select vehicle
- Pack boards (vehicle capacity)
- Estimate trip duration
- Check swell forecast at destination

### Travel Time
- Bike: real-time (short)
- Car: time skip (hours)
- Van: time skip (days for road trips)
- Boat: time skip (hours to days)
- Flight: time skip + jet lag recovery

### Fuel & Maintenance
- Vehicles need gas/charging
- Breakdowns happen (especially beater)
- Boat fuel is expensive
- Maintenance prevents breakdowns

### Board Transport Risks
- Bike: drop your board, dings
- Car with boards out window: highway dings
- Roof rack: forgot to strap, board flies off
- Flights: airline damage (protect with good bag)
- Boat: salt exposure, rinse required

## Van Life Mechanics

### Living in Your Van
- No rent (save money for boards/travel)
- Park at different beaches
- Wake up for dawn patrol
- Stealth camping vs paid campsites

### Van Amenities (Upgrades)
- Bed setup (sleep quality)
- Kitchenette (diet control)
- Solar panels (device charging)
- Water tank (freshwater rinse)
- Heater (cold climate trips)
- Roof rack capacity upgrades

### Van Life Challenges
- Finding parking/camping
- Sketchy neighborhoods
- Weather exposure
- Loneliness (mental state debuff)
- Mechanical issues

## Boat Mechanics

### Charter vs Own
- Charter: pay per trip, no maintenance
- Own: huge upfront cost, ongoing costs, always available

### Boat Trips
- Check weather window
- Fuel range planning
- Anchor at the break
- Multiple sessions per trip
- Spearfishing for food (mini-game)

### Crew System
- Hire captain (if you don't have boating skill)
- Bring friends (shared XP bonus)
- Chef (diet bonus on luxury charters)
